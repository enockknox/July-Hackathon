About Me

I am passionate about empowering youths through technology. In my community, many youths drop out of school or complete their education without clear opportunities. I believe that by training them in coding and digital skills, I can open doors to brighter futures. Tech offers a path to creativity, independence, and meaningful work. My goal is to create a safe and supportive space where youths can learn, grow, and believe in their potential. Through this journey, I hope to inspire the next generation of innovators who will lead, build, and solve real-world problems using technology.
